Egg Bake

Home

This is a delicious egg bake, and is basically the only thing that Christina could eat—that was hearty—for breakfast while pregnant.

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 3 cups spinach
  • 1.5 cups mushrooms
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 pounds breakfast sausage

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Grease glass 9" x 13" baking pan.
  3. Brown breakfast sausage. Get rid of extra grease.
  4. Mix together eggs, mushrooms, cheese, and milk.
  5. Put breakfast sausage on bottom layer of pan, followed by spinach, followed by egg mixture.
  6. Bake for 60 minutes at 350 degrees.
